What You'll Learn

  • Monitoring LLM applications for performance and quality
  • Implementing observability practices for AI systems
  • Tracking model drift and data quality issues

AI Observability is the practice of monitoring, logging, and tracing AI/ML systems to ensure they perform reliably in production. It involves tracking model performance, data quality, system health, and user interactions to detect issues early and maintain service quality.
